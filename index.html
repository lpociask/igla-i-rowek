Igła i Rowek

A local-first iPhone app for keeping track of records, listening sessions, turntables, cartridges, styli and stylus usage.

Privacy Policy Support

Private by design

Igła i Rowek stores your record collection, audio gear, listening history, stylus hours and notes on your device. The app does not require an account, does not include ads and does not use tracking SDKs.

Optional Discogs import

Discogs integration is optional and is used only when you choose to import collection data. Your local listening sessions, audio gear and statistics are not sent to Discogs by Igła i Rowek.
