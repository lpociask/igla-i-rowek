Support

How to Get Help

Contact

For support, feedback, bug reports or App Store review questions, contact:

  • Email: lpociask@me.com
  • Phone: +48 790 013 528

What to include

Please include your device model, iOS version, app version and a short description of what you were trying to do. If the issue involves Discogs import, include the visible error message, but do not send your Discogs token.

Common notes

  • The app does not require an Igła i Rowek account.
  • Discogs import is optional.
  • Most app data is stored locally on your device.
  • You can export a local backup from inside the app.
